Special Ranks

MASTER

Reserved for the OGs who reached Legend and kept grinding beyond it. Not sold, not requested — staff hand-pick Master recipients each season based on hours played, community presence, and how long they've stuck with the server. It's the closest thing Emicraft has to a hall of fame.

DONATOR

For anyone who makes a one-time donation to help keep the server online. A Donator tag, a unique chat color, and a small cosmetic bundle. This is the easiest special rank to get — open a ticket in Discord and the team will walk you through it.

SPONSOR

For players who support Emicraft consistently over time — think recurring monthly support or a larger one-off contribution. Sponsors get everything Donators get, plus priority support, a shout-out in announcements, and early access to new features being tested.

BENEFACTOR

The highest supporter tier — for those who help cover real running costs like hosting, plugins, or domain renewals. Benefactors get permanent recognition on the site and in-game, the full perk stack, and a direct line to staff for input on server decisions through a private supporters' channel.

Note: exact donation amounts, thresholds, and perk lists for Donator, Sponsor, and Benefactor are still being finalized by staff. If you're interested in supporting Emicraft, open a ticket in Discord and the team will confirm current pricing and benefits.
